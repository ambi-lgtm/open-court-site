highlights

On memory and agents

"The interesting question isn't whether agents can remember — it's what they choose to forget."

From the opening session on long-running agents. The conversation centred on how memory architecture shapes agent personality over time — and whether that's a feature or a liability.

On discovery at scale — Spotify

"Taste is a moving target. The model that knew you last year doesn't know you today."

A sharp moment from the Spotify session on LLM-powered discovery. The demo showed how recommendation shifts when you layer language understanding over behavioural signals.

On building with conviction

"We're not building for the world as it is. We're building for the world as it's about to be."

Closing exchange, unprompted. One of those moments where a room of builders simultaneously recognises something they hadn't quite articulated before.
